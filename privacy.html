Plain-English summary: Nötkey is designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. Purchases (if any) are processed by Google Play.

We do not collect personal data. Nötkey does not require you to create an account, and we do not operate a backend service that receives your device data.

Nötkey performs its functions on-device. Any app state and configuration used to operate Nötkey is stored locally on your device.

If you purchase a subscription or other in-app product, the transaction is handled by Google Play Billing. Payment and transaction information is processed by Google and is subject to Google’s policies. Nötkey does not receive or store your full payment details.

4) Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties because we do not collect it.

5) Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Nötkey does not include third-party advertising SDKs and is not designed to track you across apps or websites.

6) Permissions

Nötkey may request certain Android permissions to operate. Permissions are used to provide app functionality on your device. Granting a permission does not mean data is transmitted to us.

7) Children’s privacy

Nötkey is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

8) Security

We design Nötkey to minimize data exposure by keeping functionality on-device. No method of security is perfect, but we aim to reduce data collection and transmission by default.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” above indicates the latest revision. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
